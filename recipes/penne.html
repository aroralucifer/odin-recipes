Penne Red Sauce Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Penne Pasta
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Whipped Cream
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Take a heated pan and put some butter. Put the pasta to boil in a different burner.
  2. . Add all the chopped onion and garlic. Saute till the onion is slightly golden.
  3. Add the tomatoes.
  4. After sauting in low flame for a minute, add the whipped cream.
  5. Add the pasta sauce after a minute in low flame.
  6. Add the pasta after mixing the sauce well. Remember to add the pasta when its still hot. The pasta should still be a little undercooked.
  7. Mix the pasta with the sauce well and place the lid over it. Mix and saute in between.
  8. Remove when the pasta has absorbed the sauce.
