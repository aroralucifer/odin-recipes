Spicy Korean Noodles

Description

Ingredients

  • Nissin Geki Korean Noodles
  • Egg
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Pour about 250 ml of water in a saucer. Let it come to boil.
  2. Add Nissin Geki noodles. Don't break them.
  3. Boil an egg in a different burner. You can use a sunny side up as well. This is upto you.
  4. After 3-4 mins on high flame, turn the noodles burner off and drain out all the water.
  5. AAdd all the spice and chilli oil over the hot noodles.
  6. You can fry in a pan if you want. This step is optional.
  7. Cut the boiled egg in half and place them over the noodles. Add some cilatro. A little tiny bit.
  8. Serve Hot.
