Fried Chicken

Description

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • All purpose flour
  • Cornstarch
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Garlic powder
  • Chilli Powder
  • Black Pepper
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Clean and pat dry the chicken
  2. Season the chicken with salt and spices
  3. Mix flour (and cornstarch if using) with seasoning
  4. Whisk eggs with milk
  5. Dip chicken in egg mixture
  6. Coat chicken evenly in flour
  7. Heat oil to frying temperature
  8. Fry until golden brown and fully cooked
